General Info

Virginia Tech Smash is a community that gets together every week to play Super Smash Brothers in a friendly and competitive setting. Every week is a free tournament anyone can enter to enjoy themselves with high ranking players and casual players alike. We're also very happy to have new players We generally meet in NCB Room 120 at 8:30pm and stay till around midnight every wednesday.

Games

The Super Smash Brothers club plays every version of the game including Project M, a hacked version of the original Wii game. We also invite people to bring in their own fighting games to play as the community is very open to trying out new games every week!

New Players

We get the question of "what if im new?" a lot. That's perfectly fine! In fact, that's great! We love having new members and will happily teach you everything you need to know to have a great time and how to play a tournament set.

Don't be afraid to come by and say hi either! We welcome spectators!
